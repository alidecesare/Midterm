CERAMICS

Hand-built and wheel-spun clay pieces

This is a collection of ceramics pieces I made in high school and college. I really like being able to work with my hands to create a three-dimensional piece of art, and when I discovered ceramics, I instantly fell in love. I would consider myself a decent 2D artist, but being able to work with my hands to directly shape how something looks is the most satisfying hobby that I have.
